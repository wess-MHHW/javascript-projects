Frequently Asked Questions

What is Frontend Mentor?

Frontend Mentor is a platform that provides real-world projects for front-end developers to practice their skills. It offers a variety of challenges with designs provided, allowing developers to build responsive websites and improve their coding abilities.

How does Frontend Mentor work?

Frontend Mentor provides detailed project briefs, including design files and specifications. Developers can choose a project based on their skill level and preferences. After completing the project, developers can submit their solution for feedback and review from the Frontend Mentor community.
